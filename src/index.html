Shell

The collaboration between Ferrari and Shell is a long-term partnership that began in 1929, focusing on technological innovation and performance. Shell provides special fuels and lubricants for the Ferrari Formula 1 racing team, contributing to increased engine efficiency, speed and durability. This collaboration is not only successful on the race track, but is also applied to Ferrari production vehicles, ensuring every Ferrari car has optimal performance and high efficiency thanks to technology from Shell.
